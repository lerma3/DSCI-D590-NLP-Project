Kevin Fosnacht, Ph.D.

Dr. Fosnacht is a quantitative researcher focusing on education outcomes with over 10 years of post-doctoral experience. He has over 20 peer-reviewed publications, contributed to numerous reports, and have been frequently featured in both the popular and trade presses. Kevin served as a faculty research scientist at Indiana University, where he was part of a team administering North America's largest survey of undergraduates for about a decade. More recently Dr Fosnacht transitioned to ed-tech where he researches and assesses product impact, contributes to product development, and conducts product analytics. Kevin is currently a student pursuing a data science certificate at Indiana University, Bloomington in the Luddy School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering .

Rosemarie Lerma

Rosemarie Lerma is a doctoral student in Political Science at Indiana University, Bloomington. Her research focuses on Latino voting behavior in the U.S. Rosemarie earned a Bachelor’s degree in Political Science and Chicana/o Studies from UCLA. She earned a Master’s degree in Political Science from California State University, Northridge.
